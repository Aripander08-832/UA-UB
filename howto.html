How to play Universal Attractor

Generators

You start with 10 stars which is enough to buy your first generator. Generators generate amounts of previous tier or stars if is first. During buying generators, generators will be upgraded itself.

Prestige

Prestiging will restarts the game and make you stronger with prestige power. You can prestige for first time when you reach 10DD or 1e40 stars.

Transfer

Transfering resets prestiges and able to buy stronger upgrades. Buying all upgrades will be overpowerful and can reach Infinity.

Supernova

When you reach Infinity, the stars might overload your universe and make universe explodes. Because of that, you can supernova your stars but you lose everything you have. You get neutron stars while supernova.

Supernova Upgrades

You can upgrade to make Supernova faster and less chance to stress your body. These are cheap and cost the amount of Neutron Stars.

Bonus Achievements

You can get more achievements whenever you supernova. These achievements rewards for TP gain multiplier.

Challenges

When you reach 10 unspent neutron stars, you can do challenges. However it's harder and longer to complete. Next challenges are harder than the previous challenges as difficulty order.

Autobuyers

Each challenge rewards a type of autobuyers. You can reduce the delay with the cost of neutron stars until 0.01s have been reached.

Buyinshop

When you upgraded all of autobuyers, you can progress further with small set of repeatable upgrades. Buyinshop was a silly name.

Neutron tiers

Neutron tiers is a special type of generators which first neutron tier produces multipliers but it's worth neutron stars. You can also break 1.8e308 limit by buying your first neutron tiers.
