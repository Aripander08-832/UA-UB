How to play Universal Attractor

Generators

Generators would generates either stars or lower tiered ones. Stars is a main currency that is produced by generators which you can buy more generators.

As the bonus, you can get a x1.05 production upgrade by buying it.

Prestige

When you feel like hitting a wall, you can reset a game but you get a boost for all production called prestige power. Prestige requires e38 stars to reset and you can not get less prestige power by resetting anytime.

Transfer

There is another layer of prestige which includes to resets your prestige stuff than normal prestige. Other than prestige, you can access to upgrades but everything has a cost of different currency called transfer points.

You can get your first transfer point by reaching 1000 prestige power and you can get more by reaching higher prestige powers.

Supernova

In Javascript, there is the upper limit of all normal numbers is 1.79769313e308, which is called Infinity. But however, when you reach Infinity stars, the star amount will cause to overflow and force you to reset again, including transfer points and upgrades.

Every single supernova will give you 1 neutron stars, which you can spend on supernova content.

Supernova Upgrades

Supernova Upgrades is the first content of Supernova. These upgrades will boost your progression, including generators, prestige, and transfer content.

Bonus Achievements

There is another way to boost your progression by completing other pack of achievements. You can get it by reaching a goal but you need to supernova too. The rewards of this pack are multipliers of transfer points gain.

Challenges

Challenges is the second content of Supernova. Challenges are special progressions that you have to start over and have to reach Infinity with special conditions. But some supernova upgrades are disabled in all challenges because it's powerful that you can complete challenges easily.

Autobuyers

You can automate with an autobuyer by completing any challenge with once each. However, supernovaing will reward autoupgrader too because why not.

There is a interval waiting for your autobuyers to take action. You can reduce the interval by 20% down to 1 millisecond by buying with neutron stars.

Buyinshop

Buyinshop is a shop that involves shop features for autobuyers. After buying some buyinshop features, you notice you can supernova automatically in no time!

Some buyinshop features are expensive that you recommend to break limit!

Neutron boosts

Neutron boosts is the content that you can able to break the limit! Soon(TM)
