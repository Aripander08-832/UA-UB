How to play Universal Attractor
Some are rephrased by IkerStreamer

Generators

Generators would generates either stars or lower tiers. You can buy them by spending stars, which is a main currency of the game.

Prestige

Prestiging is a soft reset where you gain prestige power, which gives a production boost for all generators.
Prestige will be available at 1e39 stars, the more stars you have the more prestige power you will gain.

PP formula after prestiging (without buffs): ([current stars]/1e31)^0.05

Transfer

Transfering gives much like prestige, resets the game, however when you transfer you get a set amount of transfer points. These points can then be spend on upgrades.
Your first transfer point will become available at 100x prestige power, the more prestige power you have the more transfer points you will gain.

TP gain formula (without buffs): floor(([current PP]/100)^3)

Supernova

Supernova is a soft reset similar to prestige and transfer. When you reach 1.8e308 stars, you will be able to blow up your stars and cause supernova. However you will only be forced to do so when you reach 2.3e362. Each supernova earns 1 neutron star, which you can spend on supernova content.

Supernova upgrades

Supernova upgrades are similar to upgrades on transfer. These upgrades will not reset on supernova but have a cost of neutron stars. Couple upgrades which start with amounts or bought upgrades instead are called 'headstarts'.

Achievements

There is other way to boost your game by not buying upgrades but reaching special conditions. You will earn a small boost after completing an achievement than buying an upgrade, which gives a large boost.

Autobuyers

Autobuyers are automatic part of the game. All autobuyers shares the same interval that can be reduced by spending neutron stars, and can be earned by opening reward boxes. However you will earn autoupgrader after supernova.

Challenges

Challenges are similar to supernova run. These runs have special conditions and will not earn you the same rewards a regular supernova would. The goal to reach 1.8e308, when you reach this goal you will be forced to supernova.
Completing a challenge for first time earns a reward box, which requires time to open. Each reward box will contain a different auto buyer.

Buyinshop

Buyinshop features are similar to upgrades from supernova. Buying any buyinshop feature earns a feature for autobuyers.

Neutron boosts

Buying any neutron boost upgrades increases neutron boost power which gives to a production boost to all generators. One of neutron boost upgrades increases prestige power gain however.
Beside neutron boosts, you can now break limit of 2.3e362.

NS gain formula: floor(10^(x/308.254-min((x-308.254)/95020.97-1,0)))

Prestige Headstart Upgrades

Before break limit, there is simple formula for calculating the prestige power after you transfer or supernova. After that, formula changes and you can buy upgrades with cost of neutron stars to increase the calculations too.
The formula is chosen based on the minimum of the values that are calculated subformulas and there are 3 subformulas.

Normal subformula: x[neutron stars]^min(max([log10 of neutron stars],5),12.5)
Pre-break limit: x1e16
Post-break subformula: x[highest prestige power]^0.9

Neutron tiers

Neutron tiers are similar to normal generators which it costs with NS and produces neutrons instead. Neutrons are translated to cost reduction of all generators.

After reaching 1e55 NS, you can now upgrade your neutron tiers!
